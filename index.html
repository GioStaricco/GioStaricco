About

Gio Staricco is a vocalist, guitarist, keyboardist, and songwriter whose music blends alternative rock, grunge, folk, and classic rock influences into a sound that is both familiar and distinctly his own. Drawing from his Argentine-American heritage and upbringing in suburban New Jersey, Staricco's songwriting explores identity, memory, relationships, and personal growth through emotionally driven lyrics and performances. With a foundation rooted in storytelling, his work combines the raw energy of alternative rock with the introspective qualities of indie and folk music. Whether performing solo or with a full band, Gio's focus is creating songs that connect through honesty, melody, and authenticity.
